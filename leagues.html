Leagues

Hawk Pines offers a wide variety of leagues. These leagues are designed for people to be a part of a team and meet new people. Leagues are very resonably priced and offers great incentives.

League Type League Day League Time League Price
Regular Tuesday 6:00 P.M. $150
Green League: ages 50+ Wednesday 12:00 P.M. $100
Red League: Kids (ages 9-15) Monday 10:00 A.M. $75
Team Best Ball Thursday 6:00 P.M. $200 (per team)
Two-Night League Monday and Wednesday 6:00 P.M. $250

Green League

The Green League offers seniors the chance to come out to Hawk Pines and enjoy some fresh air and some great fun. It gives anyone over the age of 50 the chance to meet new people or invite friends out for a round of golf.

Red League

The Red League was designed for kids to be able to get out of the house and enjoy some time out on the course over the summer. This allows kids to play together and better their golf game. Most importantly, it allows them to have a lot of fun!

Team Best Ball

We are very excited to announce the Team Best Ball League this year. It is a brand new leagues that allows you to put a team together and compete against other teams in a head to head, best ball showdown!
